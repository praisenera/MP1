About us

PN consttruction services company is a leading builder in diverse market segments. The company has earned recognition for undertaking large, complex projects, fostering innovation, embracing emerging technologies, and making a difference for their clients, employees and community.The company is committed to meet our customer’s expectations with our services. Work with honesty, integrity and embracing changes and innovation to seek excellence in our field. We are confident that we can provide professional client-focused construction solutions. As an employee-owned company, we go above and beyond on every project and deliver on our promises with integrity. Effective planning, efficient design and quality construction are consistently applied in project planning and implementation. PN Construction Services Corporation maintains skillful, competent labor workforce who works hand in hand with every efficient site administrator to guarantee best results.

Vision

To become the leading construction company, while delivering projects that consistently exceed local standards and provide exceptional customer satisfaction.

Mission

To be a leading construction company in the global market. To become the customers' most preferred choice by attaining excellence in quality and timely completed value added projects. To provide the highest level of service in the construction industry while offering superior craftsmanship to every project, we handle. To continually innovate, develop and adopt state-of-the-art technology in methods and materials to enhance productivity and cost effectiveness. To continually improve the competence of our team, and employ diverse, innovative & results-oriented personals, motivated to deliver excellence. To build a safety culture aimed at continually reducing the frequency severity rate towards achieving zero accidents. To identify and mitigate all the environmental impacts arising from our activities, and comply with applicable environmental norms.
